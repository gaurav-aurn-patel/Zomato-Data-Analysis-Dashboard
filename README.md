# Zomato Data Analysis Dashboard
## 📌 Short Description / Purpose
This Power BI dashboard (`Zomato_Data_Analysis_Dashboard.pbit`) provides a comprehensive analysis of Zomato's restaurant ecosystem. It is designed to help stakeholders—such as restaurant owners, food industry analysts, and consumers—understand market trends, pricing strategies, customer satisfaction, and geographical food distribution. By transforming raw dining data into interactive visualizations, this project uncovers actionable insights into what drives a restaurant's success on the platform.

## ✨ Features / Highlights
* **High-Level KPIs:** Quick-glance metrics displaying total restaurants analyzed, average customer rating, total user votes, and average dining cost.
* **Geospatial Analysis:** Interactive map visuals pinpointing restaurant concentrations and rating hotspots across different cities or specific neighborhoods.
* **Cuisine & Cost Correlation:** Visualizations analyzing the relationship between popular cuisines and average pricing, highlighting the most profitable or saturated market segments.
* **Customer Sentiment:** Breakdown of restaurants by rating tiers to gauge overall market quality and customer satisfaction.
* **Interactive Slicers:** Dynamic filters allowing users to slice the data seamlessly by *City*, *Price Range*, *Online Delivery Availability*, and *Table Booking Options*.
